Getting help
============

Having trouble? We'd like to help!

* Try the :ref:`FAQ <faq-index>` -- it's got answers to many common questions.

* Looking for specific information? Try the :ref:`genindex`, :ref:`modindex` or
  the :ref:`detailed table of contents <contents>`.

* Search for information in the `archives of the rapidsms google groups mailing list`_, or
  `post a question`_.

* Ask a question in the `#rapidsms IRC channel`_, or search the `IRC logs`_ to see
  if its been asked before.

* Report bugs with rapidsms in our `ticket tracker`_.


.. _archives of the rapidsms google groups mailing list: http://groups.google.com/group/rapidsms
.. _post a question: http://groups.google.com/group/rapidsms
.. _#rapidsms IRC channel: irc://irc.freenode.net/rapidsms
.. _IRC logs: **PLEASE UPDATE  - WHERE ARE IRC LOGS -- **
